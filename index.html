BMW M PERFORMANCE

BMW M models are high-performance vehicles known for their blend of luxury and racing technology. They are developed by BMW M GmbH and are characterized by features like M sports suspension, powerful engines, and unique M tuning.

BMW M Performance goal

BMW M Performance vehicles offer a blend of high performance and sporty styling, distinct from the more aggressive M models. M Performance cars are equipped with powerful, efficient engines, like the inline six-cylinder used in the BMW 1 and 2 Series, and the V8 in models like the M550i xDrive.
